Fresh Pasta Dough

Description

Sometimes, boxed pasta just won't do. Fresh pasta is great for Bolognese, Alfredo, or with butter and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Finely milled all purpose flour (tippo 00)
  • Semolina flour
  • Egg yolks
  • Olive oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine all purpose flour and semolina flour
  2. On a clean work surface, create a mound of the flour and create a well in the center.
  3. Add the egg yolks to the well and break them apart with a fork
  4. Beginning slowly, use a fork to slowly incorporate the flour
  5. Once the egg yolks have taken on enough flour to not be too runny, move to using your hands to bring the dough together
  6. You want the dough to feel slightly dry. It will hydrate while it rests. If it's too dry add a tablespoon of water or olive oil
  7. Knead for 5-7 mintues, until pinching the dough feels like pinching your earlobe.
  8. Cover in plastic wrap and allow to rest for at least 30 minutes, up to a day in the fridge or a month in the freezer.
