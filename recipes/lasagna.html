Lasagna

Description

Gooey cheese, bright tomatoes, and big beefy flavor combine for a great lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef and Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • One whole egg
  • salt

Steps

  1. Sweat onions in olive oil
  2. Once onions are turn translucent, add garlic and allow to cook for about a minute
  3. Add ground beef and sausage and brown for about 8-10 minutes. Then set aside.
  4. Prepare a pot of salted boiling water, a large bowl with ice water, and a sheet tray lined with parchment paper.
  5. Follow the instructions for cooking the lasagna noodles "al dente" on the packaging, but take about 1 minute off the cooking time.
  6. take the noodles from the boiling water and quickly submerge in ice water, then move to the sheetpan. Set aside
  7. In a medium bowl combine ricotta, the egg, and half of the parmesan. Mix well.
  8. Assemble the lasanga in a baking dish. Begin with a thin layer of tomato sauce, then a layer of noodles. spread an even layer of the ricotta mixture on the noodles. Add another layer of noodles. Add more sauce, and cover with a layer of the ground beef and sausage. Then top with another layer of noodles. Cover with more Sauce an dthe mozzarella cheese.
  9. cook covered in a 375 degree oven for about 45 minutes. Uncover and cook for another 15-30 mintues until the cheese on top is golden brown.
